🌟 Kickstart Your Career This Summer! 🌟

It’s never too early to take charge of your future—and summer is the perfect time to get ahead! Whether you're just starting out or looking to level up, Career Success has everything you need to make this summer count.
 

✅ No resume? No problem! Choose from our curated collection of professional resume templates to get started fast.
🎯 Nail your next interview with Big Interview—practice your skills and get feedback on your resume.
💼 Explore 5,000+ opportunities on Handshake—from internships to full-time roles, your next big break is just a click away.
 

Don’t wait for opportunity to knock—go out and grab it. Your career journey starts now!

Upcoming Career Courses
Enrollment Open

We are excited to announce our updated career development courses designed to equip you with practical skills and real-world experience. Based on student feedback from the initial run, we have reworked these courses and reduced the workload to better align with your needs. Here’s a snapshot:

 

ARTSSCI 3000: Designing Your Life After College
  • Credits: 2
  • Days: Mondays, Wednesdays
  • Time: 12:40 – 1:35 pm
  • Location: Jennings 136
  • Enrollment Cap: 25 
  • Description: Connects students with career resources, tools, and alumni to create a thoughtful career plan. Includes a micro-internship, industry-specific course, or certification to enhance competitiveness.
ARTSSCI 4000: Designing Your Career Launch
  • Credits: 2
  • Days: Tuesdays, Thursdays
  • Time: 4:10 – 5:05 pm
  • Location: Hayes 024
  • Enrollment Cap: 25 (Seniors only)
  • Description: Prepares students for post-graduate careers by offering real-world experience with industry partners. Includes a comprehensive industry project to showcase on resumes, cover letters, and in interviews.

Enrollment for these courses is OPEN!
